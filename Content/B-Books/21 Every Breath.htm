Every Breath

2015

Hope Anderson finds herself at a pivotal moment in her life. At 36, she's been in a long-term relationship with her orthopedic surgeon boyfriend for six years, yet marriage seems elusive. With her father facing a diagnosis of ALS and no wedding plans on the horizon, Hope retreats to her family's cottage in Sunset Beach, North Carolina. Here, amidst the task of preparing the house for sale, she grapples with tough decisions about her future.

Tru Walls, a safari guide from Zimbabwe, receives a surprising letter claiming to be from his father, leading him to Sunset Beach for the first time. Seeking to uncover his mother's mysterious past and reclaim lost memories, Tru's journey intertwines with Hope's in unexpected ways. Despite their initial status as strangers, a powerful and inexplicable connection ignites between them.

As they spend immersive days together, Hope and Tru must confront difficult choices that challenge their sense of duty to family and their pursuit of personal happiness. Every Breath delves into the complexities of love, exploring how it can both bind us to our deepest loyalties and force us to reckon with life's regrets and enduring hopes. Ultimately, it poses the poignant question: How long can a dream endure in the face of reality?
