The Wish

2021

In 1996, Maggie Dawes's life takes a dramatic turn when she is sent to live with her aunt in Ocracoke, a secluded village on North Carolina's Outer Banks. Feeling homesick and disconnected from her old life, Maggie's perspective shifts when she meets Bryce Trickett, a local teenager with charm and ambition. Bryce introduces Maggie to the beauty of Ocracoke and ignites her passion for photography, which becomes a defining aspect of her future.

Fast forward to 2019, and Maggie has become a celebrated travel photographer, splitting her time between running a successful gallery in New York City and capturing stunning images in remote locations worldwide. However, her world is shaken when she receives a sobering medical diagnosis that grounds her over the Christmas holidays. During this time, she forms a close bond with her young assistant, finding solace in their companionship.

As they navigate the final days of the season together, Maggie shares with her assistant the story of another significant Christmas from her past—one that shaped her life in unexpected ways and led her on a journey she never could have imagined.
