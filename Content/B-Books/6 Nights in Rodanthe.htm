Nights in Rodanthe

2002

At the age of forty-five, Adrienne Willis finds herself forced to reassess her entire existence after her husband leaves her for a younger woman. Overwhelmed with heartache and seeking solace, she seeks refuge in the quaint coastal town of Rodanthe, North Carolina, where she agrees to look after a friend's inn for the weekend. However, her plans for a peaceful getaway are disrupted when a powerful storm approaches—threatening to ruin her respite.

Enter Paul Flanner, a fifty-four-year-old man who has recently sold his medical practice and arrived in Rodanthe to escape his own troubled past. As the storm looms closer, these two wounded souls find solace and comfort in each other's company. Over the course of a single weekend, their connection deepens, setting off a chain of emotions that will reverberate throughout their lives forever.
