Two By Two

2018

At 32, Russell Green's life seems like a dream come true: a beautiful wife, an adorable daughter, a successful career, and a comfortable home in Charlotte. But beneath the surface, cracks begin to appear in his seemingly perfect world. Suddenly, Russ finds himself jobless and divorced, with sole responsibility for his daughter.

Thrown into the unfamiliar role of single parent, Russ faces challenges he never imagined. As he navigates this new reality, he discovers reserves of strength and resilience he didn't know he had. Despite the difficulties, Russ's journey of single parenthood ultimately becomes a source of fulfillment and growth, pushing him to his limits and reshaping his understanding of himself and his priorities.
