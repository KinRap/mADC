The Rescue

2000

When faced with raging fires or life-threatening accidents, volunteer firefighter Taylor McAden is driven to take dangerous risks to save lives. However, there's one risk he can't bring himself to take: falling in love. Throughout his adult years, Taylor has sought out women in need of rescue, only to leave them once the crisis is over and the relationship becomes intimate.

During a severe storm in his small Southern town, single mother Denise Holton's car slides off the road with her four-year-old son Kyle inside, who has severe learning disabilities. Taylor discovers Denise unconscious and injured but can't find Kyle. As Denise wakes, they realize the chilling truth: Kyle is missing.

As the search for Kyle unfolds, a bond forms between Taylor and Denise. Taylor doesn't anticipate that this rescue will be different from previous ones, requiring more than physical bravery. It will lead him to confront his own emotional barriers and past traumas, encouraging him to embrace love and open doors to his heart that were once closed by pain. This rescue will challenge him to seize life to its fullest by daring to love.
