The Choice

2007

Travis Parker seemingly has it all: a fulfilling job, loyal friends, and a picturesque waterfront home in a charming North Carolina town. Fully embracing the pleasures of life—boating, swimming, and laid-back gatherings with his amiable companions—he holds onto the belief that committing to a serious relationship would only cramp his style. However, everything changes when Gabby Holland moves in next door.

Despite Travis's efforts to extend a friendly hand, the captivating redhead appears to harbor reservations about him, especially with her long-term boyfriend in the picture. Despite himself, Travis finds himself drawn to Gabby, and his persistent attempts to connect with her set them both on an unexpected journey.

Spanning the pivotal years of young love, marriage, and family, "The Choice" delves into the profound question: How far would you go to preserve the possibility of love?
