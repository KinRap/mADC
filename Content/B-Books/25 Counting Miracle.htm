Counting Miracle

2024

Tanner Hughes, raised by his grandparents and following his grandfather's military path as an Army Ranger, has always been a nomad, happiest when traveling on new adventures. However, after his grandmother's passing, her last wish for him to find where he belongs sparks his curiosity about his unknown father. Tanner heads to Asheboro, North Carolina, where he meets Kaitlyn Cooper, a single mother and doctor, and feels an instant connection. Meanwhile, nearby, 83-year-old Jasper lives alone in a cabin near a national forest, haunted by a tragic accident from his past. When rumors of a legendary white deer surface, Jasper becomes obsessed with protecting it from poachers. As these characters' lives intertwine, unexpected miracles may alter their futures forever.
