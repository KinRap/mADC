At First Sight

2005

Jeremy Marsh never imagined he'd leave the hustle and bustle of New York City, let alone give his heart away again after a failed marriage or become a parent. Yet, here he is, residing in the quaint town of Boone Creek, North Carolina, engaged to Lexie Darnell, the woman he loves, and eagerly anticipating the start of their family.

Just as Jeremy's life appears to be settling into a blissful routine, a mysterious and unsettling email disrupts their happiness, setting off a series of events that will challenge the foundation of their relationship. As they navigate doubts, fears about parenthood, and unexpected obstacles, Jeremy and Lexie confront the question of how well they truly know each other.

Continuing the journey of the young couple introduced in Sparks's bestselling novel "True Believer," "At First Sight" delves into the heartbreak, tension, romance, and surprises that come with the early days of marriage. It's a remarkable tale of love between partners and the bond between parent and child, highlighting the transformative power of endings that pave the way for new beginnings, tragedies that lead to unforeseen joy, and the enduring magic of everlasting love.
