Introduction

Nicholas Sparks is one of my favorite authors. I deeply admire his writing, as he has never disappointed me in his works. Sometimes, I find myself imagining that the scenarios depicted in Nicholas Sparks' novels could happen to us—a tragic romance, perhaps. In a world filled with surprises, love is omnipresent, yet true everlasting love seems elusive. Love resembles a seesaw, fluctuating like a swing, but ultimately yearning to find its steady place. Who can say what the future holds?

I'm hopeful that you'll also be charmed.
