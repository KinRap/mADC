Safe Heaven

2013

Director: Lasse Hallström

Screenplay: Dana Stevens and Gage Lansky

Cast: Julianne Hough, Josh Duhamel, David Lyons, Cobie Smulders

he film, directed by Oscar®-nominated Lasse Hallström, is based on the bestselling novel by Nicholas Sparks and follows the journey of a mysterious young woman, portrayed by Julianne Hough, who arrives in a small North Carolina town. Initially reluctant to fully integrate into the tight-knit community, she gradually begins to put down roots and finds the courage to pursue a relationship with Alex, played by Josh Duhamel, a widowed store owner with two young children.

As their romance blossoms, dark secrets from the young woman's past threaten to unravel her newfound happiness, forcing her to confront her fears and rediscover the true meaning of sacrifice. With the support of Alex and the power of love, she must navigate through the challenges that emerge, ultimately discovering strength and resilience in the face of adversity.

Featuring a talented cast including Cobie Smulders and David Lyons, this deeply moving romantic thriller explores themes of redemption, forgiveness, and the transformative nature of love. As the young woman's journey unfolds, audiences are drawn into a suspenseful and emotionally resonant story that celebrates the resilience of the human spirit.
