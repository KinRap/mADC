The Lucky One

2012

Director: Scott Hicks

Screenplay: Will Fetters

Cast: Zac Efron, Taylor Schilling, Blythe Danner

Run Time: 100 minutes

In "The Lucky One," based on Nicholas Sparks' bestselling novel, Zac Efron portrays U.S. Marine Sergeant Logan Thibault, who returns from his third tour of duty in Iraq with a photograph he believes saved his life. The image features a woman he doesn't know, but he becomes determined to find her. Learning her name is Beth (played by Taylor Schilling) and where she lives, Logan shows up at her door and ends up taking a job at her family's local kennel. Despite initial mistrust and complications in Beth's life, a romance blossoms between them, offering Logan hope that Beth could be much more than just his good luck charm.

Directed by Academy Award-nominated filmmaker Scott Hicks, "The Lucky One" explores themes of fate, destiny, and the power of love to overcome adversity. With a blend of romance, drama, and emotional depth, the film captivates audiences as Logan and Beth navigate the complexities of their relationship and confront their own past traumas. Alongside Efron and Schilling, veteran actress Blythe Danner adds depth to the cast, creating a compelling and heartfelt cinematic experience.
