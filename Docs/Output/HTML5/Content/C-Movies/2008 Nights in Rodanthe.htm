Nights in Rodanthe

2008

Director: George C. Wolfe

Screenplay: Ann Peacock and John Romano

Cast: Richard Gere, Diane Lane

In this romantic drama, Gere plays Paul, a surgeon who sacrificed his family for his career, while Lane portrays Adrienne, a devoted mother grappling with her husband's infidelity and his desire to reconcile.

Set in a remote inn on the Outer Banks of North Carolina, both Paul and Adrienne find themselves at a crossroads in their lives, seeking solace and reflection. However, as a storm approaches, they are drawn to each other for support, leading to a transformative romance that deeply impacts their lives.

Adapted from the best-selling novel by Nicholas Sparks, "Nights in Rodanthe" explores themes of love, loss, and second chances, as Paul and Adrienne navigate their personal struggles and find unexpected comfort and connection in each other's company.
